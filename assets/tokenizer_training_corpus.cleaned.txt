Mini paragraphs.
Tokenizers are compression-like systems for text. They define a mapping between strings and integer IDs. Better tokenization can reduce sequence length and improve efficiency.
A practical workflow is to collect domain text, normalize it, train several tokenizer variants, then evaluate each using downstream metrics and qualitative inspection.
When vocabulary is too small, sequences get longer. When vocabulary is too large, rare fragments waste capacity. Balance is task dependent.
